# **App Name**: PhiloThoughts

## Core Features:

- Blog Post Generation: Generate philosophical blog posts based on a given topic, adhering to a specific structure and tone. Uses AI to emulate a reflective human writer.
- Content Management: Allow creation, editing, and deletion of blog posts via a simple UI.
- Display and Formatting: Display blog posts in a clean, readable format with appropriate headings, paragraphs, and styling to enhance readability.
- Commenting System: Allow users to comment on blog posts to foster discussion and engagement.
- Tagging and Categorization: Categorize blog posts by relevant philosophical themes or topics, facilitating easy browsing.
- Search Functionality: Enable users to search for specific topics or keywords within the blog.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep purple (#673AB7) to evoke a sense of introspection and thoughtfulness.
- Background color: Light grey (#ECEFF1), nearly white, providing a clean and distraction-free reading experience.
- Accent color: Soft gold (#FFCA28), analogous to purple, to highlight key elements without overpowering the design.
- Headline font: 'Literata', a serif, for an elegant, literary feel appropriate for philosophy.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif, for clean and modern readability.
- Use minimalist icons related to philosophical concepts to enhance visual appeal.
- Maintain a clean and structured layout with generous white space to improve readability and reduce visual clutter.
